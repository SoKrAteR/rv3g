I am an 8th grade student at Riga State Gymnasium 3. After writing the 6th grade exam, I had a difficult dilemma about which school I should go to. Therefore, on this site I want to write the advantages of my school to make your choice easier.
Education
R3G has a very high level of education. Each subject is given due attention, and the teachers know their stuff and can explain the material well. Usually we have 9 lessons every day, and in my opinion this is the optimal number, because we are given less homework, and I think that it’s better to have everything explained at school than to spend several hours at home struggling with homework.
Atmosphere in RV3G is very friendly. Our teachers are very kind and know how to talk to teenagers correctly. The director of our school is generally a great person who cares about the school. He often organizes various events, talks with students and always tries to change the school for the better. And in general, as for me, RV3G is one large and friendly collective.
The area of ​​our school is simply huge. At the entrance there is a well-kept courtyard with trees and flowers. The school itself has as many as 5 floors, an assembly hall, a large gym, a swimming pool and a small room with exercise equipment. The classrooms themselves are spacious and modern, there are everything you need for all lessons.
In conclusion - RV3G is a wonderful school and an excellent choice if you want to get a good education in a pleasant atmosphere. But our school is also improving every year, and this is not the limit. Feel free to go to RV3G, we will be waiting for you!
